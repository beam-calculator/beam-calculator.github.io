  • The query already signals that appui restraint matters, which means users are usually trying to avoid the mistake of using a simply supported formule on a restrained poutre.

Questions frequentes
Why is a fixed-fixed poutre not well served by the generic poutre solveur?

Because the generic poutre solveur is tuned pour standard one-portee appui et charge cases. A restrained poutre avec fixed-end moments needs the dedicated advanced page.

What if my poutre is not perfectly fixed at both ends?

Then the exact fixed-fixed page may overstate rigidite. Use it as one bound, et compare against a less restrained case such as the propped cantilever page.

Why include the formulas page in search resultats pour a fixed-fixed query?

Because users often want to understand why the response differs from simple-portee textbook cases, even when the final calculation belongs on the advanced page.
