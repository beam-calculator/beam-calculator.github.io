Questions frequentes
Why should the converter rank ahead of the main poutre solveur pour this query?

Because the query itself signals that unit consistency is the main pain point. The poutre solveur becomes useful only after the entrees are coherent.

Can I skip the converter if I already know my units?

Yes. If your poutre entrees are already consistent, go straight to the poutre solveur or the AISC page.

What is the most courant unit mismatch in poutre checks?

A courant issue is mixing portee in meters, charge in kN per meter or kip per foot, et inertia in cm^4 or in^4 without converting them into one coherent set.
