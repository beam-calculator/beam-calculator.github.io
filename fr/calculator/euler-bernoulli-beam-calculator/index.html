Notes techniques
  • Use this page when you want the standard elastic poutre model without project-standard framing around it.
  • The model is appropriate pour slender structural members where effort tranchant deformation et large-rotation effects are not governing.
  • When continuity, partial fixity, or multiple appuis start to control the response, switch to the advanced poutre pages that use the FEA-based solveur.
