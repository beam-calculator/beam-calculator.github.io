Platform Overview

Ueber Uns

Beam Calculator ist eine statische Engineering-Plattform fuer schnelle strukturelle Vorpruefungen, klare Annahmen und getrennte Seiten fuer konkrete Beam-, Material-, Querschnitts- und Standardthemen.

Wofuer Die Plattform Gebaut Ist
  • The site is designed around individual engineering tasks rather than one overloaded interface, so users can open the calculator page that matches their structural question directly.
  • Core topics on the platform include beam deflection, load distribution, column buckling, section properties, materials, and multi-span or restrained beam behavior.
  • The visual language follows a technical blueprint style, but the goal is practical engineering clarity: fast input, transparent output, and direct internal links into related structural pages.
